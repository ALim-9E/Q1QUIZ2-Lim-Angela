Tangled

When Flynn, the kingdom's most wanted bandit, takes refuge in a mysterious tower, he meets the beautiful and magical Rapunzel. Rapunzel has been locked-away in the tower for years, and she sees Flynn as her ticket to Freedom. Together, Flynn and Rapunzel embark upon a hilarious, heart-warming, and hair-raising adventure.
Children of all ages will love reading or listen centering to this delightful adventure!
